How PostPilot works

Write once, send everywhere. Connect your accounts, compose (with AI help if you like), then schedule or post instantly — PostPilot handles each platform for you.

Connecting accounts

Bluesky & Mastodon — paste an app password (Bluesky) or access token (Mastodon) in the Accounts tab. Official APIs, the most reliable.
LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X — click “Connect via browser”, log in once in the window that opens; PostPilot reuses that session to post for you.
TikTok — TikTok blocks automated uploads; treat it as semi-manual: PostPilot prepares the video + caption and you do the final upload by hand.

Create & schedule

Tip: scheduled posts survive restarts, and each platform posts independently — if one fails, the others still go out. Click any media thumbnail to preview it before posting.
